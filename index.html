My Story

I am a recent graduate of Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering. During the last part of my college days, I found myself engaged in software development. I've seen how its products have changed the flow of the world, and I've said to myself that I want to be part of the innovative side that pours out their minds to make something for people.

In the past, I collaborated with my former classmates on a small school project. I also was trying to play with some pet projects to improve my coding abilities. While I am new to the whole process, I dream to make or take part in a project that focuses on the productivity and self-development of consumers.

In all honesty, I'm still learning more about software engineering and development. I'm trying to learn best coding practices to improve the quality of my work. I've been also reading articles and watching videos on topics such as mobile development to expand the area of expertise. I know that through constant practice and patient hard work I will be able to deliver quality products in the future.

Projects

ERIS Management Information System (Thesis Project)

A computer-based management information system for Eulogio Rodriguez Integrated School developed collaboratively using C#.Net. The system is capable of performing functional tasks such as Enrollment, Class Scheduling, Grading System, Record Maintenance and Generation of Statistical Reports.

Tags: C#.Net, MySql Database

Motivational Blogsite with CMS (Training Project)

A simple website featuring a compilation of motivational materials designed using HTML, CSS, PHP and MySql Database. This project comes with its own CMS, which allows the admin to update the content posted on the website.

Tags: HTML, CSS, PHP and MySql Database

Preloved Bookstore Website with  CMS (Underdevelopment)

A pet project for a Preloved Bookstore. The website is capable of showcasing variety of books, enabling users to search book collections and to reserve books for later pick-up. This website has a content management system that is accessed by the admin. The project is currently being developed using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, AJAX, JSON, PHP and MySql Database.

Tags: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, AJAX, JSON, PHP and MySql Database
